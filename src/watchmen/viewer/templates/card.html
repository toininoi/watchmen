How the rating works

Each axis is normalized 0–1 against a tunable cap. The overall rating is a weighted average mapped to 40–99 so even a fresh corpus gets a respectable card. Archetype comes from the dominant axis when one is clearly ahead, otherwise Generalist. The card SVG is stateless — refresh anytime to see your stats now.
